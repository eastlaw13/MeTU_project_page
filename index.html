Project Overview

MeTU (MobileViT-based U-Net) is designed to bridge the gap between high-accuracy segmentation and edge feasibility. By combining MobileViT with U-Net style skip connections, MeTU leverages global representation while maintaining spatial precision on resource-constrained edge CPUs.
