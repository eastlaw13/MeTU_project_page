The Idea behind MeTU 💡

"How can we run high-quality segmentation on a tiny Raspberry Pi?" This question led to MeTU. By combining MobileViT (which sees the big picture) with U-Net skip connections (which catch the fine details), we've built a model that's light enough for edge devices but sharp enough for industrial tasks.

Real-world Robustness 🌦️

MeTU isn't just for sunny days. Even in fog, rain, or snow (Cityscapes-C), our model maintains steady performance.

How fast & accurate? 📊

🏆 Better than Segformer-b0

MeTU-xs outperforms Segformer-b0 in accuracy (mIoU) while using 45% fewer parameters. It's the perfect balance between 'Small' and 'Smart'.
